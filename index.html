About Me

I am a PhD student at the Programming Languages group at Delft University of Technology. My research focuses on specification of static semantics. My work is implemented in the meta-language NaBL2, which is part of the Spoofax Language Workbench. I am being supervised by Prof. Dr. Eelco Visser.

If you want to know more about my research, have a look at my talks and publications, or contact me.
